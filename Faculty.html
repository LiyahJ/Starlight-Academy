Introducing our Headmistress & our professors:

Our Headmistress, Mizuki Kanzaki, she is coutinuing the legacy of her predecessors. She has been the headmistress of Starlight Academy for 40 years. She wishes that all of her students can be successful in their idol activites, & bring joy to not only themselves, but to those who watch them either in concert or through the screen feels joy as well. She hopes to see all of those who wish to join the academy acheive their dreams even if this is not the right path for them.
Ms.Hime, our singing instructur, she will work your singing muscles to the core & make it so you can sing steady & clearly across the stage. She has been at our instition teaching for 12 years, she still puts on concerts while teaching her students & strongly advises her students to attend them. Her moto is: "Always work the hardest behind the scences, so everyone will believe you are flawless!"
Ms.Yozara, our syling instructur, her goal is to help students coordinate their outfits & makeup to match the theme the student wants to convey to their audience. She has been working at our institon for 16 years, while teaching she still does her modeling career with her favorite brand Spicy Ageha.
Ms.Tsubasa, our acting instructur, she wants her students to be able to cry on cue with tears running from their eyes, but they must be able to turn it off just as quickly & invoke emotion from those that watch them. She has been with the academy for 18 years & has appeared on many popular shows like "I wanna be a rockstar" & if you look in to the old archives of the school she was the star for our in school news show. She is currently working on a new show called "Detective Flora".
Ms.Yuzu, our dancing instructur, she wants her students to always be entergetic & through their dancing make their audience feel entergetic themselves just from watching them on stage.
